Tequila Lime Chicken

Juicy grilled chicken breasts are infused with a mouthwatering blend of
tangy, smoky, sweet and lightly spicy flavors. Tequila Lime Chicken is
Southwestern cuisine at its finest!

Ingredients

4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/4 cup tequila
juice of 1 lime (about 2 tablespoons)
2 tablespoons lime zest
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon honey
1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
1/4 teaspoon ancho chili powder
1/4 cup of olive oil
salt & pepper to season

Instructions

Make Marinade: To a small bowl add tequila, lime juice, lime zest,
garlic powder, cumin, honey, smoked paprika, ancho chili powder,
salt, pepper and olive oil. Whisk to combine.

Marinate Chicken: To a large ziplock bag add chicken breasts. Pour
marinade over the chicken breast and seal. Let chicken marinate for
at least 20 minutes
Heat grill to medium heat, about 350-375Â°F.

Place marinated chicken on the grill. WARNING: there is tequila in
the marinade so it is going to flare up as soon as you put it on the
grill.

Grill each side for approximately 4-6 minutes.

Remove from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing. Garnish with cilantro and lime wedges.
