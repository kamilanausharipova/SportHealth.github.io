Mushroom Spinach Frittata

Filled with fresh spinach, crispy bacon bits, juicy cherry tomatoes, thinly
sliced mushrooms and creamy gruyere cheese, this Mushroom Spinach
Frittata is perfect for breakfast or brunch. It comes together in under 30
minutes!

Ingredients

12 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup chopped fresh spinach
5 strips cooked bacon, roughly chopped
1/2 cup thinly sliced mushrooms
1/3 cup halved cherry tomatoes
1/3 cup shredded gruyere cheese

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350°F.

Spray a 15×10 baking sheet with cooking oil. (you could also use 17×10
if needed)

To a large bowl add eggs, milk, garlic powder, salt & pepper. Whisk
together and pour into prepared baking sheet.

Top with spinach, bacon, mushrooms, shredded cheese and then
cherry tomatoes. Be sure to evenly spread them across the sheet
pan.

Bake for 14-16 minutes until eggs no longer jiggle.

Remove from oven. Let rest for 2-3 minutes. Cut and serve.
